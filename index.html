Crop Price Prediction Dashboard

The Department of Consumer Affairs monitors daily prices of 22 essential food commodities through 550 price reporting centers across the country. Strategic market interventions stabilize price volatility using buffer stocks of pulses (gram, tur, urad, moon, masur) and onion.

Price trends are analyzed based on seasonality, historical and emerging trends, market intelligence, and production estimates. ARIMA-based economic models are also employed for forecasting prices.
